# Super-Resolution Survey

> A full-text survey of papers from [ChaofWang/Awesome-Super-Resolution](https://github.com/ChaofWang/Awesome-Super-Resolution).
> **All 2026 papers** plus **landmark historical papers** were read directly (one research agent per paper) and written up below.
> ✅ = surveyed in full · ✅* = surveyed (paper text not fully accessible, grounded in abstract + domain knowledge).

**Coverage:** 35 papers from 2026 + 27 landmark papers = 62 total.

- 📚 Combined cross-paper analysis & trends: **[SuperResolution_2026_Report.md](SuperResolution_2026_Report.md)**
- 📂 Per-paper full reports: **[`papers/`](papers/)**
